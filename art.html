Visual Arts

Ink has been a favorite medium of mine since I first picked up a pencil. As a child, I was always drawing, and even though I'm busy now, I still like to draw! Here are just a few of my favourite art pieces.

Sketches

Pachinko

This artwork portrays the story of Noa in "Pachinko", a Korean immigrant who lived in Japan from 1939 to 1978. The piece has a dark and depressing tone, which is meant to evoke a sense of discomfort and convey the feelings of despair Noa experienced growing up.
